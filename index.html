About Me

I’m Aharon Rabson, a Master’s graduate in Data Science (MSc, Bellevue University) with a passion for extracting actionable insights from data. My work bridges exploratory analysis, predictive modeling, and impactful visualization across health, education, climate, and AI domains.

My journey into technology began in high school, where I spent three years immersed in IT studies—learning Java (OOP) and SQL, and ranking in the top 1% of the country. That experience sparked a lifelong love for coding and problem-solving.

I have a particular interest in the medical and mental health fields, but I remain fascinated by any area where data science can make a difference. I’m always eager to tackle new challenges and help drive positive impact through data.
